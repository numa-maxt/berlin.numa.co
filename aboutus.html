About NUMA Berlin

NUMA Berlin is a startup hub producing growth and innovation programs for entrepreneurs and corporates. We are part of the global network of NUMA, reaching from Berlin and Paris to Mexico, New York City and beyond.

Our Mission

NUMA empowers mission-driven tech entrepreneurs to solve the global problems of 2030. We want to support impactful innovations especially in the Energy, Mobility, Health, Food and Education sectors as well as Artificial Intelligence & Blockchain as key-technologies.
